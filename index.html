About

As a seasoned cyber risk strategist with over a decade in the trenches, I specialize in building resilient security programs that align with executive-level business objectives. My approach is rooted in transforming security from a cost center into a strategic business enabler.

My career has been defined by leading high-stakes initiatives for Fortune 500 financial institutions, where I've architected robust risk frameworks, automated compliance and reporting, and served as a trusted advisor on multi-million dollar technology transformations. I excel at demystifying complex technical risks for C-suite audiences and driving enterprise-wide cultural shifts towards proactive risk management.

I am passionate about not just identifying risks, but also building sustainable programs that enhance organizational resilience. My goal is to serve as a trusted advisor, enabling businesses to innovate and grow securely in an ever-evolving threat landscape.

Leadership Philosophy

I believe effective security leadership is built on three pillars: Clarity, Collaboration, and Empowerment.
  • Clarity: Translating complex security challenges into clear, actionable strategies that resonate from the boardroom to the development team.
  • Collaboration: Fostering a culture where security is a shared responsibility, breaking down silos between technology, risk, and business units.
  • Empowerment: Equipping teams with the autonomy, resources, and strategic direction they need to innovate securely and take ownership of their domains.
